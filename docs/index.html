✈️ Travel-aware dosing

Plan a trip and Doseify ramps your dose times toward destination time — a little each day, keeping every gap safely even, then eases you back home.

⏰ Reminders that keep up

Dose reminders fire at the right moment even when your phone changes timezone mid-flight, with gentle follow-ups until you log or skip.

⌚ Apple Watch

See today's doses on your wrist and log them with one tap — reminders on the phone and Watch stay perfectly in step.

🔒 Private by design

No account, no cloud, no analytics. Your medications and history live only on your iPhone and Apple Watch.

📊 Adherence at a glance

Streaks, weekly adherence, and a shareable PDF report for appointments with your doctor.

💊 Refill nudges

Track how many doses you have left and get a heads-up before you run out.

Doseify is a personal organisational tool, not a medical device. Dose timing suggestions — including travel adjustments — are indicative only. Always confirm your medication schedule, and any changes to it, with your doctor or pharmacist.
